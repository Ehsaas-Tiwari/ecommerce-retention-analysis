## Business Context

The executive leadership team identified a concerning decline in customer loyalty.  
The company's **current average retention rate is 72.84**, while the **industry target is 85**.  
This gap directly impacts long-term revenue, marketing efficiency, and brand stability.

---

## Dataset

| Quarter | Retention Rate |
|----------|----------------|
| Q1 | 67.7 |
| Q2 | 72.8 |
| Q3 | 76.09 |
| Q4 | 74.75 |

✅ **Correct Average:** **72.84**  
✅ **Industry Benchmark Target:** **85**

---

## Key Findings

- Retention improved from **Q1 → Q3**, indicating short-term campaign effectiveness.
- A **decline in Q4** suggests weakening loyalty entering the next fiscal year.
- The company is currently **12.16 points below industry benchmark**.
- At current levels, long-term customer lifetime value (CLV) is at risk.

---

## Business Implications

- Reduced retention increases:
  - Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
  - Marketing inefficiency
  - Revenue volatility
- Lower loyalty weakens:
  - Subscription renewals
  - Cross-selling opportunities
  - Brand advocacy
- If the current trend continues, the company risks significant market share loss.

---

## Strategic Recommendation (Required Solution)

✅ **Implement targeted retention campaigns**

These should include:
- Personalized offers for high-risk churn segments
- Loyalty rewards for repeat customers
- Subscription incentives
- Predictive churn modeling
- Region & behavior-based marketing automation
